
DEqueue(doubly ended queue)
insertion and deletion both occurs from both front and rear
incase of insertion from front , push the element in the front index and then front--
types of DEqueue-
    restricted i/p-insertion from front is not allowed
    restricted o/p-deletion from rear is not allowed
Data is same as queue
operations-
    isEmpty()
    isFull()
    enqueueF()
    enqueueR()
    dequeueF()
    dequeueR()
    initialize()
    print()
